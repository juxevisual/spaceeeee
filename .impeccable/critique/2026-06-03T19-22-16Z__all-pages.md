## Anti-Patterns Verdict

Not AI slop. Double-bezel architecture, ghost-indigo surface tint, per-section identity colors, and functional innovations (velocity arrows, frequency counts, spending pace) are distinctive. Stat cards approach SaaS hero-metric zone but double-bezel saves them.

## Overall Impression

Coherent private tool with genuine identity. Portfolio and Expenses most polished. Notes and Dates have rougher edges around tap targets and state feedback. Biggest opportunity: error recovery and feedback.

## What's Working

1. Ghost-indigo surface tint creates unmistakable cohesion across all surfaces.
2. Category velocity + frequency in expense bars gives multiple signals on one scan line.
3. Per-section color identity in navbar (amber/Notes, sky/Dates) is purposeful and uncommon.

## Priority Issues

**[P1] Together "Personal total" label semantically wrong** — shows combined total of both users, not just logged-in user. Fix: rename to "Combined personal."

**[P1] Note card action tap targets below 44px on mobile** — five icons at p-1.5 (~28px). Fix: increase to p-2.5 or collapse secondary actions into overflow menu.

**[P1] No success feedback for delete operations** — no toast on delete across all pages. useToast() exists but unused for deletes. Fix: add toast for all delete operations.

**[P2] Past one-time dates sort to bottom and become invisible** — daysUntilNext for past non-recurring dates returns large positive. Fix: separate upcoming/past sections with divider.

**[P2] Author bubbles identical for all users** — all amber regardless of who wrote the note. Fix: primary user = indigo, partner = gain green, matching Together avatar system.

## Minor Observations

- Custom expense categories show raw key instead of label in "Top category" stat.
- "More" tab shows "More" even when on Notes/Dates routes.
- Reaction bar appears on your own notes (self-reaction is odd).
- Exchange rates show no staleness warning beyond timestamp.
